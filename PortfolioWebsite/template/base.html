About Me

I am a third-year undergraduate student at the University of California, Davis, majoring in Applied Statistics with an emphasis in Psychology and minoring in Chinese.
My anticipated graduation date is in June 2018.

Throughout my college career, I discovered my passion for statistics and fell in love with the idea of getting hold of this vast data our society has to offer and analyzing the given information to make some sense out of it.

My interests are driven towards the fields of data analysts, data mining, and data science in particular. Statistics is a rapidly developing field, and with more access to raw data found all over the world, and my dream is to be able to mine them and analyze it to get a better understanding of this world, whatever the subject matter is.

Outside of school, I am currently interning at an IT company located in Tokyo, Japan. I work remotely from the U.S. with an intern partner in Japan to build a website using html, css, python, django, javascript, and sql, representing the Japan trade statistics. Through this internship, I am hoping to attain more knowledge in programming as well as hands on experience analyzing real world data.

Where I am from:
I was born in Japan, grew up in Hawaii, and moved to California to attend UC Davis. Besides my passion for statistics, I am a public relations officer for the Japanese Language and Culture Connection Club (JLCC) and a travel coordinator officer for Boxing club. I am also an athlete who competed for boxing. These extra-curricular activities help shape me as a person and builds me to become a strong, independent person.

Hobbies:
As you might have guessed from reading the above paragraph, I love boxing and exercising in general. Besides boxing, I have also started bouldering. I love reading books (classics, fiction, non-fiction, fantasy, etc.) but I try not to read when school is in session to prevent myself from getting to immersed in those books. I also love traveling and have some amazing stories both exhilarating and somewhat scary stories, which I would love to share with you, if you are interested.

Goals:
My goal at the present is to soak in as much statistical knowledge as possible so I would actually be able to apply my knowledge after I graduate. Therefore, I am reaching out to anyone who is willing to help me gain more knowledge and experience! Thank you.
